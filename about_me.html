Hello everyone this is a web-application on blogging and social networking.
In this web-application you can post anything you want.But for this you need
to have registered account in this web-app. You can register here by simply
going to newusers tab in the homepage. When you will enter at the first you
have to login here, if you dont have an account in this web-application then
you have to create one. If you don't want to post and only want to read global
posts then simply click on the homepage.

If you want to delete account then go to the log-out tab in the my-profile page
page. You can also update your password if you forgot the password or want to
update it time-by-time.

You can also search people by their username by typing their name in the search
bar in the homepage. You can visit people's profiles and read their posts.
In the Homepage there is a Search tab , if you are reading a post and found a
word whose meaning is unknown to you then simply go to the search tab and write
the url of that word and it will give you all the details about that word.
